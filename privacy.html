Defta Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 24, 2026 · Last updated: May 24, 2026

Defta ("we", "us", "the app") is designed with privacy as a default. This Privacy Policy explains what data Defta handles, how it is used, and your rights. By using Defta, you agree to this policy.

1. Data we collect on our servers

None. Defta does not operate a backend server. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, account information, contact details, or device identifiers on servers operated by us. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, marketing trackers, or crash-reporting services in the app.

2. Data that stays on your device

The following information is created and stored only on your iPhone and is never transmitted to us:

  • App-block selections and Screen Time configuration, managed by Apple's Family Controls / Managed Settings framework.
  • Your quest progress, exercise counts, scroll budget, and streaks.
  • Camera and microphone input used for on-device exercise counting (push-ups, squats, speaking practice). Video and audio frames are processed locally by Apple's Vision and Speech frameworks and are never recorded, uploaded, or persisted.

3. Subscriptions and third parties

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple via In-App Purchase. We use RevenueCat (RevenueCat, Inc., USA) to verify and manage subscription status. Apple shares an anonymous user identifier and purchase event metadata with RevenueCat — we do not receive your name, email address, Apple ID, or payment information.

RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. Permissions the app requests

  • Screen Time / Family Controls — to block selected apps when your scroll budget is empty.
  • Camera — to count exercise repetitions locally on-device.
  • Microphone — to support speaking-practice exercises locally on-device.
  • Notifications — to remind you about quests and budget status.

All processing happens on your device. You can revoke any permission at any time in iOS Settings.

5. Children's privacy

Defta is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, and certainly not from children under 13. If you believe a child has somehow provided information, contact us and we will assist — though there is no data on our servers to delete.

6. Your rights under GDPR (European users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Because Defta does not collect personal data on its servers, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete. Uninstalling the app removes all on-device data.

7. Your rights under CCPA/CPRA (California users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information, and to non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

We do not collect, sell, or share any personal information. No personal information has been collected or sold in the preceding 12 months.

8. Data retention

Because we do not store any personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to retain. On-device data exists only as long as the app is installed and is removed when you delete the app.

9. International transfers

No personal data leaves your device. Apple and RevenueCat may process subscription metadata in the United States and other countries — see their respective privacy policies for details.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
